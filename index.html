N1 - Unsubscribe

Unsubscribe from any marketing email without leaving N1. The plugin will first check if it can automatically unsubscribe. If not, it will open up a mini browser window and allow you to quickly unsubscribe. Once done, the email is trashed and you can take a step towards email productivity!

1. Download the plugin here and unzip

2. From the N1 menu, select "Developer" > "Install a Plugin Manually" and select the unzipped folder that you download

3. Get to unsubscribing!
